Bio

Discipline in every layer.

I have been making music since I was eight, growing from a young musician into a self-taught producer with a sound shaped by discipline and experience. After years of building independently, I returned to college to pursue Music Production and Audio Engineering. Before stepping fully into music, I served in the United States Marine Corps, and that same grit and precision now fuel everything I create.
